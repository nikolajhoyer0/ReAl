Selection ?

Usage:
    Selection is used to produce a subset of the tupples from the original realation.

Notation:
    In general: ? Condition Relation
    Using the relation R(name, phonenumber, adress) with the following tupples:
        {Daniel, 123456, first avenue}
        {Nikolaj, 654321,  Second street}
        {Tobias, 165234,  Third boulevard}
        ? name = 'Daniel' R
    Will create a relation with only the tupple {Daniel, 123456, first avenue}.
